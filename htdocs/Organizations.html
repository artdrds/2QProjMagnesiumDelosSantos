Leading Research Organizations for Climate Change

World Wildlife Fund

Contributions

  • Helping transition developing countries to clean energy sources like wind and solar
  • Collaborating with US Latino leaders and communities to raise awareness of climate risks from Latin America to North America
  • Curbing climate pollution from international aviation

“Our mission is to conserve nature and reduce the most pressing threats to the diversity of life on Earth.”

350.org

Contributions

  • Fossil Fuel Divestment Campaign
  • International Day of Climate Action
  • Summer Heat Campaign

“350 is building a future that’s just, prosperous, equitable and safe from the effects of climate change.”

Greenpeace

Contributions

  • Promote renewable energy as a solution that can power the world
  • Nurture peace, global disarmament and non-violence
  • Initiate the campaign to stop the planet from warming beyond 1.5º to prevent the most catastrophic impacts of the climate breakdown

“Greenpeace exists because this fragile earth deserves a voice.”

The Climate Reality Project

Contributions

  • Hosts an annual event called 24 Hours of Reality, a 24-hour live broadcast about the climate crisis and its solutions with a one-hour segment in every time zone
  • Climate Reality Leadership Corps
  • Trained selected members of the public to give public talks focused on the harmful effects of climate change and ways to address climate change at the grassroots level

“Our mission is to catalyze a global solution to the climate crisis by making urgent action a necessity across every level of society.”


Other Organizations


Why are these organizations important?

These organizations all aim to do three main things: Understand the causes and consequences of Climate Change, educate and inform people about Climate Change, and initiate the creation of activities and laws that will help fight Climate Change. All these allow us to stand a chance against the continuous rise in temperatures. Furthermore, these organizations keep track of the changes that's happening in our environment. This is very crucial, for it allows us to grasp a portion of how complex the situation really is. It allows us to see the reasons why we have to act now. Additionally, these organizations have raised awareness to people around the world, and in turn, many governments and individuals have started to take action to fight Climate Change. There are even laws passed that would limit the carbon emissions of certain countries. It is so important that we listen and take note of these organizations because it will help us know how we can contribute to the right side of this battle against Climate Change.
