Ways to Help

Do

  1. Switch to LED lightbulbs
  2. Use energy-efficient electric appliances
  3. Save Energy at Home/Workplace
  4. Plan and Combine Trips
  5. Eat more vegetables
  6. Consider walking, taking the bike or public transport
  7. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Repair
  8. Educate yourself and Raise Awareness
  9. Drive a fuel-efficient car
  10. Lessen your carbon footprint
  11. Donating to Climate Change Organizations

Don't

  1. Drive cars that emit dark/gray smoke
  2. Throw away plenty of leftovers or trash
  3. Burn garbage
  4. Litter
  5. Promote deforestration
  6. Keep electronics/appliances on when not in use
  7. Keep car engine on when on standby
  8. Pretend that Climate Change isn't real

Why take action now?

Like what was said in the other parts of this site, the temperatures are, in fact, rising. At this point, one can say that it doesn't matter if this is caused by Climate Change or not. The idea is that there is an evident increase in the temperatures worldwide, and it's affecting the life on planet Earth in a concering way. For this very reason, we must continue to gain awareness about the topic and do something to help fight the change that's causing our environment to slowly crumble before our very eyes. Let us not listen to concepts that deny Climate Change, for many of these reasonings simply dismiss the consequences of the rising temperatures. Animals and Plants on Earth are dying. There is no denying that Earth now is different from the Earth decades and centuries ago. It's so crucial that we take action now because everyday has become a chance for the situation to get worse. It will take time before we see the catastrophic damages of Climate Change, but even now, traces of this are showing. From rising sea levels, some species going extinct, melting glaciers and ice in polar regions, more unpredictable weather conditions, to weather phenoma, the consequences of Climate Change are slowly revealing themselves, and anyone with common sense would know that no one will survive if all these consequences are combined together to be made 100x worse than it already is now. Should we really wait for this day to come? No. We must do something to help our planet. It has taken care of us by providing us our resources that sustain our wants and needs, it is only right that we take care of it in return. We shouldn't sit and wait for things to get so much more dangerous and catastrophic. We must do something NOW.
